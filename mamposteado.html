Mamposteado

Mamposteado is a traditional dish from Puerto Rico.

Ingredients:

  • Pink Beans
  • White Rice
  • Butter
  • Salt
  • Spices

Steps:

  1. Cook the beans on the stove, and add the spices to you're liking.
  2. Cook the rice and add a little salt and butter.
  3. Mix the rice and beans together.
